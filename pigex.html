Welcome to Pig Manager – Your Smart Farming Assistant


Swine farming has long been a vital part of agriculture in Northern Mindanao, significantly contributing to the region's economy. Despite its importance, many farmers face challenges in efficient management due to outdated methods and limited access to tailored digital solutions. Pig Manager addresses these issues by providing an integrated mobile platform for accurate expense tracking, health management, and seamless operations.

Empowering swine farmers in Northern Mindanao and beyond

Our Pig Manager



Our app is designed based on the feedback of local farmers, ensuring practical features that resonate with everyday farm needs:


  • ‣ Offline Functionality: Work seamlessly without an internet connection.
  • ‣ Expense Monitoring: Track every cost, from feed to hidden expenses like employee wages.
  • ‣ Health Tracking: Log vaccination schedules, pregnancies, and herd health events.
  • ‣ Easy Record Management: Printable and downloadable reports for organized data management.
  • ‣ User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive and easy to use for farmers with any level of tech experience.
